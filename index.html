General Information

This repository collects representative technical writing samples spanning Simplified Technical English (STE) editing, S1000D-conformant Interactive Electronic Technical Manuals (IETMs), fault isolation procedures, illustrated parts catalogs, and systems operator guides — drawn from 15+ years writing DoD- and industrial-compliant documentation for programs including the RQ-4 Global Hawk, EMALS/Ford-class carriers, and commercial aircraft maintenance publications.

Sample — Hypothetical / Non-Proprietary

Every sample below was authored independently by Nathan Smith as a demonstration of technical writing method and format conformance. None contain classified, export-controlled, or proprietary data — all part numbers, drawings, and technical values are fictional. Each PDF carries its own disclaimer on the cover page.
